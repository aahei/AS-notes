### 2.1 Algebra
#### Glossary
- **modulus** *a.k.a.* **absolute value**
- **polynomial**
- the **degree** of the polynomial
	*highest power of $x$*
- **dividend**, **divisor**, **quotient**
- **remainder**, **factor**
